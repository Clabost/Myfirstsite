Lego DC

Every so often I tend to go back to older projects and work on them from scratch. This gives me a fantastic oportunity to truly see the progress I have accomplished over the years.

With this project I revisited an old piece I did in 2014 when I was starting to play with Cinema4D. Eight years and one Batman The Lego Movie later, I came up with this piece. I really hope you enjoy it as much as I did working on it.

All models were downloaded from Mecabricks site, whome I can not thank enough for the fantastic job they do. Once downloaded, the scene was assembled in Cinema4D and some pieces needed retopology for optimal bending, so zBrush was my go to for this part of the process. Textures, shaders and lights were worked with Redshift Render and final compositing done in Adobe Photoshop.

Please enjoy!
