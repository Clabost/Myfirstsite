Bionicle: Matoran Adventures

The game takes place on the fictional island of Mata Nui, which is called a "tropical paradise" with six thematic regions[1]. Each region (Ga-Wahi water region, Le-Wahi Jungle region, Ko-Wahi ice and mountain region, Onu-Wahi Underground region, Po-Wahi Desert region and Ta-Wahi Volcanic region) makes up one of the six levels of the game. The game follows a Matoran who tries to repel an invasion by a swarm of Bohroks. In the official Bionicle story, bohrok defeats Toa.

Matoran Adventures is a side—scrolling platformer with puzzle elements. The player can choose one of three difficulty levels (Easy, Medium and Difficult).

The game uses a character change mechanic similar to The Lost Vikings. If necessary, the player can switch between Matoran and Turaga to overcome obstacles. By collecting all the parts of a certain mask, they get the opportunity to play as another Matoran character. One Matoran character, Kongu, is available from the beginning; the other five Matoran characters must be unlocked.
